Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Grill the chicken breasts until cooked through and slice them.
  2. In a bowl, mix the greens, cucumber, and tomatoes.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the salad and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Top the salad with the grilled chicken slices.
  5. Serve with your favorite dressing.
