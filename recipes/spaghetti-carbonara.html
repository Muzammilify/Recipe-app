Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta with garlic until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs with grated Parmesan and pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix it with the pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the egg mixture until creamy.
  6. Serve hot with extra Parmesan on top.
