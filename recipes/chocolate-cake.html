Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine all dry ingredients.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the wet ingredients.
  4. Gradually combine both and stir until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased pan and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Let it cool before serving. Optionally, frost with chocolate icing.
