Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a wok or large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for a minute.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender but crisp.
  4. Add soy sauce and stir to coat the veggies.
  5. Sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired and serve hot.
